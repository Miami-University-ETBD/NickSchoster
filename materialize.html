About the Game

Bug Bouncers is a fast-paced, game where bugs must knock each other into the grass to pop their bubbles. Only the last bug standing wins!

Game Features

Feature Description
Floaty Movement Master floaty and bouncy movement mechanics to stay alive!
Bubble Combat Knock your opponents into the grass to eliminate them.
Multiplayer Action Compete with friends and test your bouncing skills.
