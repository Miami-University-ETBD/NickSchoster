How to Play

In Bug Bouncers players must learn to control their movements to defeat their enemies. The goal is to outmaneuver your enemies and bounce them into the grass while keeping your own bubble safe.

The game features two unique levels with different platform layouts. If the player is pushed into the grass, they lose and must restart the level.

Game Rules

  • Objective: Knock all enemy insects into the grass to clear a level.
  • Survival: If you are knocked into the grass first, you lose and must restart the level.
  • Level Progression: Defeat all enemies in one level to advance to the next.
  • Platforms: Navigate the platforms strategically to gain the advantage over your enemies.

Gameplay Breakdown

Feature Details
Levels Two unique battle arenas with different platform layouts.
Physics Bouncy movement and collisions to create a dynamic experience.
Winning Eliminate all enemies to progress to the next level.
Losing If you touch the grass first, the level resets.

Tips & Strategies

Use momentum wisely! Time your bounces to knock enemies off balance while keeping control of your movement.
