About the Game

Bug Bouncers is a fast-paced platforming and momentum-based game where players control an insect that is trapped inside a bubble. Players must use strategic movement to outmaneuver their opponents and knock them into the grass to pop their bubbles.

The player's survival depends on skillful bouncing! If your bubble touches the grass first, you lose, and the game restarts. The mechanics are fairly simple to get down quickly. You must be the last bug standing.

Game Mechanics

Understanding the mechanics of Bug Bouncers is key to victory!

  • Movement: Floaty and bouncy movement allows players to have chaotic and quick maneuvering.
  • Combat: Bounce into enemy insects to knock them into the grass.
  • Death: If you touch the grass first, your bubble pops, and the game restarts.
  • Win Condition: Eliminate all enemy insects by bouncing them into the grass.
Feature Description
Controls Easy to learn movement mechanics with floaty motion.
